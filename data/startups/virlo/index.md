## Problem

Short-form video content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) dominates social media, but creators and marketers have no reliable way to track what's working. Guesswork leads to wasted time and missed opportunities.

## Solution

Virlo provides real-time social intelligence and performance data for short-form video campaigns. It eliminates guesswork by showing what content performs best across platforms.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Content strategists, media buyers, agency owners
- **Secondary:** Creative directors, growth leads, solo creators
- **Use cases:** Campaign optimization, trend analysis, competitor research
