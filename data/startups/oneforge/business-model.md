# 1Forge - Business Model

## Overview

Foreign exchange market data API for developers who need currency rates in apps and trading workflows.

## Target Market

Developers, fintech apps, dashboards, trading tools, and SaaS products with currency conversion needs.

## Pricing Model

Usage-based API tiers. Free sandbox, then $9-99/mo by requests, symbols, and websocket access.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $8,479
- **Customers:** 173
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. List in API marketplaces and developer directories.
2. Publish currency conversion examples for popular languages.
3. Target fintech builders and spreadsheet automation users.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
