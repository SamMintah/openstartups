# 1Forge

## Problem

Developers need reliable FX rates without negotiating with banks or data vendors. 1Forge packages currency data behind a simple API.

## Solution

1Forge packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Developers, fintech apps, dashboards, trading tools, and SaaS products with currency conversion needs.
- **Team Size:** Small team
- **Use cases:** Aggregate exchange-rate data from licensed or partner data feeds.
