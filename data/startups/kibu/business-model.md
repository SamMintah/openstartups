# Kibu — Business Model

## Overview

EHR and compliance software for organizations serving people with intellectual and developmental disabilities (I/DD). 48 states.

## Target Market

Healthcare orgs, I/DD providers


## Revenue

- **MRR:** $234K
- **Category:** SaaS
- **Growth:** Steady


## Growth Strategy

1. Pick a single compliance pain point (progress notes)
2. Get 3 pilot orgs signed up at $0
3. Charge when you save them 5+ hrs/week
4. Expand to billing + EHR modules
