# Sync for YNAB - Business Model

## Overview

Bank feed syncing utility for YNAB users who need smoother account imports in unsupported markets.

## Target Market

YNAB users, personal finance enthusiasts, and budgeters in markets with weak native bank-feed coverage.

## Pricing Model

Low-price subscription around GBP 3-8/mo. Offer annual plans because the value is ongoing convenience.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** GBP615
- **Customers:** 170
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Reach YNAB communities with helpful setup guides.
2. Rank for bank sync and YNAB import keywords.
3. Build comparison pages for supported banks and countries.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
