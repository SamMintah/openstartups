# Sync for YNAB

> Bank feed syncing utility for YNAB users who need smoother account imports in unsupported markets.

**Website:** [https://syncforynab.com](https://syncforynab.com) | **MRR:** GBP615 | **Source:** [Baremetrics Open Startups](https://syncforynab.baremetrics.com)

---

## Problem

YNAB users outside fully supported bank-feed markets often waste time importing transactions manually. Sync for YNAB sells convenience by connecting bank data to budgets.

## Solution

Sync for YNAB packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** YNAB users, personal finance enthusiasts, and budgeters in markets with weak native bank-feed coverage.
- **Team Size:** Small team
- **Use cases:** Integrate with a regulated bank data provider and map accounts to YNAB budget accounts.
