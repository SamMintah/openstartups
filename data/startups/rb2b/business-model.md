# RB2B - Business Model

## Overview

B2B visitor identification for sales teams. Turns anonymous website traffic into company and contact-level prospecting signals.

## Target Market

B2B SaaS companies, outbound sales teams, demand generation teams, and agencies that need visitor identification.

## Pricing Model

Usage-based or seat-based plans. Start with a free trial, then $99-499/mo based on identified companies and integrations.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $771,529
- **Customers:** 3,663
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Rank for visitor identification and website intent keywords.
2. Partner with outbound agencies and RevOps consultants.
3. Publish teardown content showing missed pipeline from anonymous traffic.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
