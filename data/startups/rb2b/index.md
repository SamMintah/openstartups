# RB2B

> B2B visitor identification for sales teams. Turns anonymous website traffic into company and contact-level prospecting signals.

**Website:** [https://rb2b.com](https://rb2b.com) | **MRR:** $771,529 | **Source:** [Baremetrics Open Startups](https://rb2b.baremetrics.com)

---

## Problem

B2B teams get useful website visits but cannot see which companies are researching them. RB2B turns anonymous traffic into sales-ready account signals so teams can follow up while intent is fresh.

## Solution

RB2B packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** B2B SaaS companies, outbound sales teams, demand generation teams, and agencies that need visitor identification.
- **Team Size:** Team
- **Use cases:** Add a lightweight tracking script that identifies visiting companies from IP, firmographic, and enrichment data.
