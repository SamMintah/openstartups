# MoltSets - Business Model

## Overview

Niche subscription software with a small but paying customer base, reported publicly through Baremetrics.

## Target Market

Niche professional users with a repeat workflow that is underserved by broad horizontal software.

## Pricing Model

Simple monthly subscription. Start at $19-49/mo and use high-touch onboarding to prove value.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $1,935
- **Customers:** 15
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Direct outreach into the niche community.
2. Create templates that mirror the spreadsheet people already use.
3. Ask early customers for referrals inside the vertical.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
