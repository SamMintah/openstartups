## Problem

Very niche operators often need workflow software that broad SaaS tools ignore. MoltSets shows how a focused product can still support paid subscriptions with a narrow customer base.

## Solution

MoltSets packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Niche professional users with a repeat workflow that is underserved by broad horizontal software.
- **Team Size:** Small team
- **Use cases:** Interview a narrow user group and map the recurring workflow they currently run in spreadsheets.
