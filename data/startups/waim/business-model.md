# Wandering Aimfully - Business Model

## Overview

Membership and education business for creative entrepreneurs building calmer, more intentional online businesses.

## Target Market

Creative entrepreneurs, solo founders, coaches, designers, and online business owners.

## Pricing Model

Membership or annual community. Charge $49-199/mo or $500-1500/year depending on coaching depth.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $14,840
- **Customers:** 149
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Build in public through founder essays and transparent lessons.
2. Run free workshops that teach one useful business system.
3. Use member outcomes as case studies.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
