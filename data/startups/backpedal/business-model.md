# BackPedal — Business Model

## Overview

GPS tracking and 24/7 recovery for bikes/e-bikes. Hardware + recurring subscription. 55% MoM growth.

## Target Market

Cyclists, e-bike owners


## Pricing Model

BackPedal uses a **hardware + subscription** model:

| Component | Price | Details |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| GPS Tracker | $99 one-time | Hardware cost |
| Monthly Sub | $9.99/mo | 24/7 monitoring & recovery |


## Growth Strategy

1. Source GPS tracker hardware (Quectel module ~$15)
2. Build iOS/Android app for real-time tracking
3. Charge $99 hardware + $9.99/mo subscription
4. Partner with bike shops for in-store distribution
