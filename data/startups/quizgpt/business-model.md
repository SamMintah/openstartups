# QuizGPT — Business Model

## Revenue Model

Monthly subscription with two tiers.

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | $0 | Limited attempts |
| Premium | ~$5/mo | Up to 150 quiz answers |
| Ultra | ~$10/mo | Up to 1,000 quiz answers |

## Revenue Breakdown

- **MRR:** $337
- **Total Revenue:** $1,967
- **Active Subscribers:** 86
- **Founded:** May 2025

## Growth Strategy

1. **Word of mouth** — Students share with friends
2. **Social media** — TikTok/YouTube demos
3. **SEO** — Target "Kahoot bot" keywords
4. **Referrals** — Free tier drives adoption
5. **Content** — Tutorial videos

## Competitive Advantages

- AI-powered (not just templates)
- Chrome extension (easy to use)
- Free tier available
- Simple pricing
