## Problem

Teams want analytics and marketing automation without heavy surveillance, cookie banners, or complex enterprise tooling. Friendly packages privacy-first marketing tools as a managed service.

## Solution

Friendly packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Privacy-conscious companies, European organizations, nonprofits, and marketing teams with compliance constraints.
- **Team Size:** Small team
- **Use cases:** Choose one privacy-first workflow: analytics, email automation, CRM, or consent-friendly reporting.
