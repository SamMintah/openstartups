# Friendly - Business Model

## Overview

Privacy-friendly marketing and analytics services with hosted tools for organizations that want compliant alternatives.

## Target Market

Privacy-conscious companies, European organizations, nonprofits, and marketing teams with compliance constraints.

## Pricing Model

Managed service subscriptions. Price by traffic, contacts, seats, or support level.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** CHF6,440
- **Customers:** 60
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Rank for privacy-first and GDPR-friendly alternatives.
2. Sell to agencies that need compliant tooling for clients.
3. Publish migration guides from Google Analytics and mainstream marketing suites.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
