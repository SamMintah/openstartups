## Problem

People forget to follow up after important emails. A lightweight reminder and sequence tool can make personal outreach more reliable without a full sales suite.

## Solution

If No Reply packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Founders, freelancers, recruiters, sales reps, and professionals who send important one-to-one emails.
- **Team Size:** Small team
- **Use cases:** Build Gmail/Outlook integration that detects sent emails and reply status.
