# VoiceType — Business Model

## Revenue Model

Subscription-based with usage tiers.

## Pricing (Estimated)

| Plan | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | $0 | Limited minutes |
| Pro | ~$19/mo | Unlimited transcription |
| Business | ~$49/mo | Team features, API |

## Growth Strategy

1. **Content marketing** — Voice-to-text use cases
2. **SEO** — Target "voice typing" keywords
3. **Partnerships** — Integrations with tools
4. **Free tier** — Drive adoption
5. **Word of mouth** — Professional networks

## Competitive Advantages

- Professional-focused (not consumer)
- Technical vocabulary
- Smart formatting
- High accuracy
