# Super

## Problem

Creators and small teams like writing in Notion but need a public website with custom domains, SEO, and polished design. Super turns Notion into a site backend.

## Solution

Super packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Notion power users, creators, startups, agencies, and small businesses that want fast website publishing.
- **Team Size:** Team
- **Use cases:** Connect to Notion pages and render them as public routes.
