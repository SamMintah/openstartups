# Super - Business Model

## Overview

Website builder that turns Notion pages into fast, polished websites with custom domains and themes.

## Target Market

Notion power users, creators, startups, agencies, and small businesses that want fast website publishing.

## Pricing Model

Subscription per website. Use a free preview tier, then $12-29/site/mo for custom domains and advanced features.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $96,486
- **Customers:** 5,137
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Rank for Notion website and Notion templates keywords.
2. Build a template gallery with creator examples.
3. Partner with Notion consultants and template sellers.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
