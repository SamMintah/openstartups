# Simple Analytics — Business Model

## Overview

Privacy-first web analytics. Simple, GDPR-compliant alternative to Google Analytics.

## Target Market

Developers, privacy-conscious businesses


## Pricing Model

Simple Analytics uses a **flat subscription** model:

| Plan | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Starter | $9/mo | Basic analytics |
| Business | $19/mo | Full features |
| Enterprise | Custom | High-volume |


## Growth Strategy

1. Build sub-1kb tracking script with no cookies
2. Show pageviews, referrers, top pages — nothing else
3. Charge $9/mo, offer open source lite version
4. Market via GDPR fine news and developer communities
