# Codédex — Replication Guide

> How to build a gamified coding education platform for Gen Z

## Overview

Codédex is a learn-to-code platform built specifically for Gen Z with a gamified curriculum. $87K MRR, Rank #19. The core insight: traditional coding courses are boring and not designed for how Gen Z learns. Gamification (XP, streaks, badges) keeps them engaged.

**Key Metrics:** MRR: $87K | Rank: #19 | Growth: +2% MoM | Team: Small team

## Market Opportunity

**Niche:** Gen Z coding education — gamified, bite-sized, community-driven.

**Why underserved:** freeCodeCamp and The Odin Project are great but feel like homework. Codex makes learning feel like a game. Gen Z has different attention patterns and motivation triggers.

**Competitive Landscape:** Codecademy (dry), freeCodeCamp (free but dense), Duolingo (language, not code). Codédex fills the "fun + educational" gap.

## MVP Build Guide

### Core Features
1. One language track (Python recommended — easiest to teach)
2. Duolingo-style: XP, streaks, badges, leaderboard
3. Bite-sized lessons (5-10 min each)

### Tech Stack
| Layer | Tool | Cost |
|-------|------|------|
| Frontend | Next.js | Free |
| Backend | Node.js | Free |
| Database | PostgreSQL | Free tier |
| Payments | Stripe | 2.9%+$0.30 |

**Build time:** 6-8 weeks | **Cost:** $50-100/mo

## Pricing Strategy

$9.99/mo after 7 free lessons. Annual plan at $79/yr (33% discount). Student discount: $4.99/mo.

**Positioning:** "Learn to code like you play a game. XP, streaks, and real projects."

## Customer Acquisition

1. **TikTok/YouTube:** "I learned Python in 30 days" — show progress and projects.
2. **Student ambassadors:** Give free access to college coding club leaders.
3. **Partnerships:** Bootcamps and CS departments as supplementary material.

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Content creation is the bottleneck.** Each lesson takes 10-20 hours to create. Plan for 6 months of content before launch.
2. **Gamification must be genuine.** Gen Z sees through fake engagement. Make XP actually mean something.
3. **Competition from free resources.** Your edge is curation and experience, not content独家.

- [ ] Implement XP, streaks, badges

### Launch
