# Grokability - Business Model

## Overview

Company behind Snipe-IT, an open source IT asset management platform with hosted subscriptions and support.

## Target Market

IT managers, operations teams, schools, nonprofits, MSPs, and companies managing physical hardware.

## Pricing Model

Open source self-hosted core plus hosted subscriptions. Charge for managed hosting, backups, support, and compliance features.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $240,734
- **Customers:** 5,703
- **Source:** Baremetrics Open Startups

## Growth Strategy

1. Win search traffic for IT asset management templates and open source alternatives.
2. Make self-hosting documentation excellent so admins trust the product.
3. Convert self-hosted users when maintenance becomes annoying.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
