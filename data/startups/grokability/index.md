## Problem

IT teams need to track laptops, licenses, accessories, repairs, and assignments, but spreadsheets break as soon as a company grows. Snipe-IT gives them an open source asset system with a paid hosted path.

## Solution

Grokability packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** IT managers, operations teams, schools, nonprofits, MSPs, and companies managing physical hardware.
- **Team Size:** Team
- **Use cases:** Build asset inventory with categories, serial numbers, assignments, and audit history.
