> Open source publishing and membership platform for creators, newsletters, and independent media businesses.

## Problem

Independent publishers need publishing, newsletters, memberships, and payments in one place without handing their audience to closed social platforms. Ghost packages open source publishing with managed hosting.

## Solution

Ghost packages this workflow into a focused subscription product with clear onboarding, billing, and repeatable customer value.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Creators, newsletter operators, independent publishers, media startups, and businesses with owned-audience strategies.
- **Team Size:** Team
- **Use cases:** Build publishing primitives: editor, posts, themes, tags, and SEO metadata.
