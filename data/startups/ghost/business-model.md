# Ghost - Business Model

## Overview

Open source publishing and membership platform for creators, newsletters, and independent media businesses.

## Target Market

Creators, newsletter operators, independent publishers, media startups, and businesses with owned-audience strategies.

## Pricing Model

Open source core plus hosted plans. Price by publication size, staff seats, and newsletter volume.

## Revenue Snapshot

- **MRR:** $895,920
- **Source:** Ghost official metrics

## Growth Strategy

1. Own search around creator publishing, newsletter platform, and Substack alternatives.
2. Use open source community as trust and distribution.
3. Publish transparent metrics to build credibility with publishers.
4. Keep the product narrow enough that onboarding and support stay manageable.
