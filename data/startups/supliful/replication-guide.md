# Supliful — Replication Guide

> How to build a platform for creators to launch CPG brands

## Overview

Supliful is an all-in-one platform for creators to launch CPG (consumer packaged goods) brands — product selection, e-commerce, fulfillment. No upfront costs. $191K MRR, Rank #8. The core insight: creators want physical product lines but lack supplier/logistics relationships.

**Key Metrics:** MRR: $191K | Rank: #8 | Growth: +2% MoM | Team: Team

## Market Opportunity

**Niche:** Creator-to-CPG pipeline — white-label products for influencer brands.

**Why underserved:** Starting a physical product brand requires supplier connections, minimum orders, logistics, and compliance. Creators have audiences but not supply chain expertise. Supliful bridges that gap.

**Competitive Landscape:** Shopify (DIY), Printful (print-on-demand only), Teespring (merch only). Supliful is category-focused: supplements, beverages, skincare.

## MVP Build Guide

### Core Features
1. Product catalog with white-label manufacturers
2. Brand customization tool (logo, label, packaging)
3. Shopify integration for storefront creation

### Tech Stack
| Layer | Tool | Cost |
|-------|------|------|
| Frontend | Next.js | Free |
| Backend | Node.js | Free |
| E-commerce | Shopify API | Free tier |
| Payments | Stripe Connect | 2.9%+$0.30 |

**Build time:** 6-8 weeks | **Cost:** $100-200/mo

## Pricing Strategy

No subscription fee. Margin on each sale (wholesale → retail spread). Creators get products at wholesale, sell at retail. You take 20-30% margin.

**Positioning:** "Launch your product brand. Zero inventory. Zero risk."

## Customer Acquisition

1. **Creator partnerships:** DM 100 micro-creators (10K-100K followers). Offer free brand setup.
2. **TikTok/Instagram:** "How I launched my supplement brand in 48 hours" — show the process.
3. **Shopify App Store:** List as a Shopify app for organic discovery.

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Physical logistics are complex.** Partner with experienced 3PLs. Don't try to handle fulfillment yourself.
2. **Quality control is your responsibility.** If the manufacturer ships bad product, the creator blames you.
3. **Customer service scales fast.** Every creator = potential support tickets. Build self-serve tools first.

## Launch Checklist

### Pre-Launch
- [ ] Partner with 2-3 white-label manufacturers
- [ ] Build product catalog and customization tool
- [ ] Integrate with Shopify

### Launch
- [ ] Onboard 10 creators with free setup
- [ ] Post launch content on TikTok/Instagram
- [ ] Submit to Shopify App Store

### Post-Launch
- [ ] Expand product categories
- [ ] Add subscription/recurring order support
- [ ] Build creator analytics dashboard
